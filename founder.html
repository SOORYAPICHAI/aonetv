Founder

Angelique Monét who holds the Royal Nobility titles of (Her Royal Highness Princess Dame) based upon her lineage, humanitarian efforts, and contribution to the arts, is an American multimedia talent and former Ms. Black South Carolina USA. Monét attended St. Augustines University graduating with a degree in mass communications. Monét is also the first stage actress and ventriloquist. In 2001, she performed on Theater Row her one-woman Off-Broadway show “Multiple Me” written by Edgar Chisholm, where she portrayed several personalities and her ventriloquist figure had multiple personalities in addition to those Monét portrayed. This changed modern day ventriloquism at the start of the millenium. She is one of the “most famous Angelique ever,” and “top 5 sir name Monet along with Jean Claude Monet.” Her image is now seen as a “work of art,” by major corporations from the United States, Europe, Asia to the Middle East the specific image is “Angelique Monet beauty talent,” Beautiful beauty talent Angelique Monet in black dress.

EARLY LIFE.

Angelique Monet was raised in Greenville, South Carolina and was raised by her Mother and Grandparents. She suffered from a learning disability and did not talk until the age 5. Once she began talking she was behind in school and was placed in temporary special education by the fifth grade. Through piano classes within one year Monet was ready in some levels for high school. She mentions being bullied which is why she also in this time learned ventriloquism which would later be her art form in pageantry. Through ventriloquism, Monet found her voice. Coming from a lineage of African Americans in politics, Monet is the Grand-daughter of Senator Theo Walker Mitchell of Greenville, South Carolina who was the second African American in the United States of America to receive a major party nomination (Democratic for Governor) and Bishop Johnnie Smith the first African American Republican elected official and first African American co-chairman of the Republican Party of South Carolina often known as “advisor to the Presidents.” She is also related to many pioneers to American politics and civil rights leaders.

HISTORICAL ACHIEVEMENTS.

As of August 2019 she was made a “Living Legend,” by the prestigious “Historical Black Colleges and Universities” for her contribution to “The Arts.” She has also received honors endorsed by Former Prime Minister Theresa May for her “contribution in the arts” and during the 72nd United Nations Sessions for her contributions on “World Peace through the Arts & Pan African Icon.”

START OF CAREER.

She began her career at the age of 17 through internships at the Urban League and worked her way to being a journalist and has been on-air/ behind the scenes at NBC, CBS, UPN, PBS, and syndication of her own television children’s show in top markets by the age of 20. She then went onto writing and acting in her own productions through her company Greta Joanne Entertainment. Films have been distributed world wide theatrical and digitally while premiering at prestigious festivals, including Festival de Cannes. She has acted along side Hollywood legends Angel Salazar (Scarface, Carlitos Way), Golden Globe Nominee Steven Bauer (Scarface, AMC’s Breaking Bad), and Latin mambo sensation Tito Puente Jr. In addition to helping in promotions and distribution of films with Danny Glover, Isaiah Washington, Academy Nominee Graham Green, Academy Winner Jeff Bridges, just to make a few. Greta Joanne Entertainment now has licensing to programming, is a sales agent, event production company, and is in pre-production stages to over 30 projects in various genres. Seeing the need to assist minorities in global distribution, and exposure, she formulated the Hamptons Black International Film Festival. In 2011 she Founded Artisan Festival International World Peace Initiative, a global movement to utilize all components of the arts to help in World Peace Relations. The organization is now within the outreach campaign of 'New Generation In Action' a United Nations approved NGO with ECOSOC Consultative Status. The group aides in humanitarian efforts in the promotion of film, fine art, fashion, music, in addition to youth and women empowerment campaigns to promote world peace, cultural diversity, underserved and underrepresented people, and economic stability through the arts.

PIONEER IN DIGITAL TECHNOLOGY.

Monét is also Founder of Nothing On Television TV, a Video On Demand digital platform which first launched on DISH Satellite TV. She has interviewed top tier veterans such as Academy Award Winner Morgan Freeman, Golden Globe Winner Alan Alda, Music Mogul Russel Simmons, David Morse just to name a few. The platform was launched in 2011, and was one if the first OTT (SVOD, AVOD, PPV) set ups owned by a woman and specifically an African American woman. Monet is no stranger to technology having led a group of 30 Account Executives at the start of her career with RCN the first residential bundle deal to arrange fiber optic in bundle deals. Monet states, “I sold the future and was 120 percent over quota the first month. Never would I have thought technology would go this far. It is an honor to witness such and say I’m a part of this in a forever growing marketplace. Seeing the need to help the arts and entertainment industry during COVID-19 Monet Founded AM ONE TV and AM ONE FESTIVAL

MUSIC CAREER.

The multi-media talent is also a singer and composer with an album on iTunes/ digital platforms and has performed on stage with Grammy Winner Brian McKnight, comedians Sinbad, legendary Paul Mooney with ventriloquist figure ‘Lil Licious, creators of Sesame Street with “Gracie the Pig,” and sang with Indian musician and dancer Chevalier Dr. Raghunath Manet. Monét has also performed for the United Nations Women Guild at the United Nations Headquarters as a singer and ventriloquist for the Women’s Bizarre under the Patronage of Catrina Marques de Almeida Vaz Pinto. Other performances include “Namaste Festival Paris) at Petit Palais, Off Broadway NYC, the Grand Lodge of the Free and Accepted Masons of the State of New York, events during Festival de Cannes, Luxembourg Parliament in front of European Commission Chairman Jean-Claude Juncker, United Kingdom House of Parliament not limited to.

PUBLIC SPEAKER.

Monét is also a public speaker who has spoken at the African Union Headquarters in New York City with Former United Nations Ambassador His Excellency Tete Antonio, and Her Excellency Amb. Fatima Mohammed, the United Nations for Gandhi Day, Children’s Day, and International Day of Peace. She has also spoken at Schools, Universities, and churches. She has appeared on panels with diplomats, celebrities, politicians, and humanitarians. Her dialogue specializes in world peace relations, indigenous preservation, self esteem, women's empowerment, and her days of being diagnosed with a learning disability and being placed temporary in special education courses, advocate for depression awareness and conservation. As a conservationist she has assisted in event planning, marketing and or public relations for influencers: Socialite Georgina Bloomberg (Hamptons, USA Hamptons Classic), Christie Brinkley, Rockefeller’s (Global) Rothschilds (Global) film promotions, and Madame Isabelle Kocher former CEO of energy company ENGIE (NYC Headquarters United Nations).
