Welcome to AM ONE TV

The soft launch of "AM ONE TV" & "AM ONE FESTIVAL" will take place on June 21-27, 2020 during Marché du Film (Virtual Market) and will highlight smart phone cinema, and films of purpose through a partnership with New Generation In Action's AFI World Peace Initiative (A United Nations supported organization with Consultative Status) and affiliates "to be announced soon." Unique weekly events will follow.

More About AM ONE TV

What is AM ONE TV ?

AM ONE TV will highlight short content made by smart phones by content providers around the world. Content will be delivered to consumers through smart phones, tablets, video game consoles, and smart tv. Content will range from social awareness, children, documentary, daily essentials, to drama. Additional services will include live streaming of concerts, comedy shows, and e-learning platforms. The platform is owned by Greta Joanne Entertainment created by award winning multi-media talent Angelique Monet.

Why AM ONE TV ?

AM ONE TV is more than the “Smart phone Netflix” we are the “Tesla of Modern Day Cinema” and making unique content available while also providing a sustainable platform for live event streaming for performers, e-learning classes and a global virtual festival. By joining you will be a part of a movement designed to utilize culture, accessibility, and technology to create “One New Global Family.”

VIRTUAL FESTIVAL.

AM ONE FESTIVAL was Founded by award winning multi-media talent Angelique Monet during COVID-19 as an effort to provide opportunities for the arts community during COVID-19. The"Virtual Arts Festival," will combine all artistic mediums with an emphasis on "film." In Addition live events will include virtual art galleries led by Emerging, Mid career and Established fine artist. Musuems and galleries will also participate in the virtual experince. The events will be broken down year round and also will be comprised of innovative performances such as comedy shows, theatre performances, concerts, virtual fashion shows, innovative live performances, and an e-learning platform all within a "virtual festival experience."
