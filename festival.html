Festival

AM ONE FESTIVAL was Founded by award winning multi-media talent Angelique Monet during COVID-19 as an effort to provide opportunities for the arts community during COVID-19. The "Virtual Arts Festival," will combine all artistic mediums with an emphasis on "film." In Addition live events will include virtual art galleries led by Emerging, Mid career and Established fine artist. Musuems and galleries will also participate in the virtual experince. The events will be broken down year round and also will be comprised of innovative performances such as comedy shows, theatre performances, concerts, virtual fashion shows, innovative live performances, and an e-learning platform all within a "virtual festival experience." Physical destinations will be announced in the near future. Film Submission We are a partner with Film Freeway. All films submitted with a Gold Membership are eligible for discounts.
