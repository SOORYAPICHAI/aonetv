FESTIVAL GOAL.

The overall goal of the "AM One Festival" is to bring artist together highlighting films as a main medium in a professional platform designed to increase the profile of films and filmmakers selected. We have partnered with Institutions such as New Generation In Action's AFI World Peace Initiative which holds Consultative Status to the United Nations, other NGO's and festivals in order to help expand the independent artist career and find additional resources to support artist during COVID 19.

COVID-19 FUND.

The creation of a (AM ONE DURING COVID-19) is a designated Relief Grant to assist the artist community that are not eligible for relief packages in their respective countries. We plan to cater to small NGO's and organizations with specific targets we can report back to our AM One Community with results. Addition grants will help children in distant learning who may be deprived of the proper smart phone tablets, or advantage, and we can help facilitate cloth masks for health care workers and individuals in compromising locations have the proper hand sanitizers and masks. The fund will also include New Generation In Action's AFI World Peace Initiative's Model Plan.
